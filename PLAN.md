# cafezinho

Cafezinho is a native MacOS app that prevents computer from going to sleep.

Reference apps: Caffeinate, Taurine

Functionality:
When launched, the app is shown as an outlined white cup icon in the menu bar.
On click it activates `no_sleep` functionality:
* prevents the OS from sleeping (both in charging and battery mode) indefinitely until switched off
* toggles the icon to the filled amber cup icon with three raising steam curves above it.

When in active mode, clicking on the icon deactivates `no_sleep` functionality, the icon toggles back to outlined white cup, the system goes to sleep according to its settings.
